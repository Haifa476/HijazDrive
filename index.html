Explore Our Cities

Discover the holy cities and beautiful destinations we serve with our comprehensive guides

Mecca - The Holiest City in Islam

Explore the sacred sites, rich history, and spiritual significance of Mecca, the birthplace of Prophet Muhammad and the destination of millions of pilgrims each year.

Masjid al-Haram

The largest mosque in the world and home to the Kaaba, the most sacred site in Islam. Millions of pilgrims visit each year for Umrah and Hajj.

Book Transportation

Jabal al-Nour

The Mountain of Light where Prophet Muhammad received his first revelation. It houses the Hira cave, a significant historical site.

Book Transportation

Masjid e Taneem

Also known as Masjid Aisha, it is a miqat (station) where pilgrims enter the state of Ihram before performing Umrah.

Book Transportation

Medina - The Radiant City

Explore the holy city of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), its sacred sites, and rich Islamic heritage that has attracted millions of visitors for centuries.

Al-Masjid an-Nabawi

The Prophet's Mosque, built by Muhammad (PBUH) himself. It is the second holiest site in Islam and contains the Green Dome where the Prophet is buried.

Book Transportation

Quba Mosque

The first mosque built in Islamic history. Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "Whoever purifies himself in his house then comes to Masjid Quba and prays in it has the reward of Umrah."

Book Transportation

Mount Uhud

The site of the Battle of Uhud where early Muslims fought against the Quraysh. It contains the graves of Hamza and other martyrs of the battle.

Book Transportation

Taif - The City of Roses

Explore the beautiful mountain city known for its pleasant climate, rose gardens, and rich cultural heritage in the heart of Saudi Arabia.

Al Hada Mountain

A scenic mountain region with breathtaking views, cable car rides, and cool climate. The mountain road from Mecca to Taif is one of the most beautiful drives in Saudi Arabia.

Book Transportation

Taif Rose Plantations

Visit the famous rose farms where the precious Taif roses are grown. These roses are used to make exquisite rose oil and perfumes known worldwide for their quality and fragrance.

Book Transportation

Al Shafa Mountains

A beautiful mountain village offering stunning views, pleasant weather, and traditional markets. It's the perfect escape from the heat of the lowland areas.
